About Us

We are thinking of our customers at this very moment. We will enhance the value of not my company but ours. We hope to meet you and start a wonderful relationship.

We were established as a shipping agency company in 1995 in Busan, Korea. Since then, we have accomplished a remarkable growth in the field of the shipping agency and now became one of the most outstanding agency company in korea especially with the ship's owners in the far-eastern part of Russia.

WE DO BUSINESS IN FAITH

The first and most important is keeping our words. To protect our mutual trust, we review documents over and over again. This is the way how we have run this business and surivived until now.

We have been through a lot of problems like ship's fires, burglaries, crew problems and so forth. But we deal with them all. That means we have got tons of experiences which can arrange all sorts of states of emergency.

We mainly handle marine products especially King Crab and Snow Crab. But we endeavor to expand business items. Feel free to contact us and ask us anything. We can find a happy medium.
